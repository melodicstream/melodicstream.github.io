Psychic Connections

https://rookfluffy.itch.io/psychic-connections

Psychic Connections is a gay, romance-focused furry-themed Visual Novel with NSFW elements. Follow the story of a coma patient trying to regain his footing after moving away for college; shortly after arriving, he meets several individuals with unique psychic attributes, among whom he may find a deeper connection depending on your choices.

FVN Bot

https://github.com/melodicstream/FVNBot

FVN is a management bot for the Furry Visual Novels Discord server. It maintains a database of all the furry visual novel games that there are, along with ratings. You can see it in action by joining the FVN Discord: https://discord.gg/GFjSPkh

FVN Husbando Picker

https://melodicstream.ml/fvn-husbando-picker

FVN Husbando Picker is a silly ranking application. It allows you to choose between two furry visual novel games characters at a time, and after enough rankings it displays a list of your top rated characters. Word of warning: it will always be outdated, because new furry visual novels are coming out faster than I can update the website.
